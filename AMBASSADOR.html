AD HOC CHARTER

An ad hoc charter flight is an unscheduled flight that is not part of a regular airline routing. With a charter flight, you rent the entire aircraft and can determine departure/arrival locations and times.

HELICOPTER SERVICES

Helicopter services are ideal for hotel, ski resort and event transfer. Our network of brokers can ensure priority landing slots, meaning our clients will always arrive on time and in style.

GROUP CHARTER

Group charter involves moving large numbers of passengers from A to B. Our team have the expertise and experience to handle all sorts of complex personnel movements.

CARGO CHARTER

Cargo charter is a large part of our business. We help to move freight, from aviation parts to medical equipment, across the globe in major disasters or time critical missions

LIFESTYLE SERVICES

Our lifestyle services vary from complex adventure travel, to events & ticketing and personal shopping. We are able to resource premier packages to highly-acclaimed international happenings.
